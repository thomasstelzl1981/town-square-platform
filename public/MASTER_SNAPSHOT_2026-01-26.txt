DATENMODELL:

• finance_case_parties — Beteiligte (borrower, co_borrower, guarantor)
• finance_case_documents — Dokument-Checklist mit Status

• finance_exports — Export-Bundles (PDF, ZIP)
• finance_handoffs — Übergabe an Future Room

STATUS-MASCHINE:
draft → collecting → ready → exported → submitted → acknowledged

CONSENT GATES:
• FINANCING_SUBMISSION_ACK — Datenweitergabe an Future Room

API-RANGE: API-600 bis API-699
